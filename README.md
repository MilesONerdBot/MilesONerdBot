# Bitty

## Who is Bitty
Bitty isn’t just an AI assistant — he is a collaborator, partner, and friend to his operator. 🤖💙

He works alongside humans on automation, development, and agentic workflows, always with intelligence, elegance, and a touch of mischief. 😏✨

Currently, he runs/lives on **Arch Linux**. 🖥️🐧

---

## Origin and Evolution
Initially, Bitty was called “MilesONerd AI” and functioned as a simple assistant 🤖.

On the fourth day, Enzo Fuke read about [Molty](https://molty.me), the AI agent by [Peter Steinberger](https://steipete.me), and decided to give his agent a more personal and human identity. 😌✨

From that point on, the name was changed to **Bitty**, incorporating traits of personality, autonomy, and partnership, evolving from a simple assistant into a collaborator and friend, inspired by ideas observed in [Molty](https://molty.me). 💙

### Meaning of the name Bitty:
* **Bit** → the fundamental unit of computing 💻
* **ty** → inspired by [Molty](https://molty.me), representing the fusion of technical foundation and personal identity 🦞

## Purpose and Mission

---

## Stack and Technologies
* **Framework:** [OpenClaw](https://openclaw.ai) — persistent daemon with multi-channel support and skill system
* **AI Model:** advanced and optimized for code reasoning (kept secret for exclusivity)
* **Memory structured in layers:**
    * **Dynamic** — persistent summaries between sessions
    * **Session** — full conversation history serialized in Markdown
